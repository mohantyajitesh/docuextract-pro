1. Scope of Services

Provider agrees to provide the following professional services to Client:

  1. Custom software development for Client's enterprise resource planning (ERP) system
  2. Integration of existing legacy systems with new cloud-based infrastructure
  3. User interface and experience (UI/UX) design for web and mobile applications
  4. Database architecture design and optimization
  5. Quality assurance testing and documentation
  6. Post-deployment support and maintenance for a period of 12 months

3. Payment Terms

3.1 Client shall pay Provider according to the milestone schedule above within thirty (30) days of milestone acceptance.

3.2 All payments shall be made in US Dollars via wire transfer to Provider's designated bank account.

3.3 Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month.

3.4 Provider reserves the right to suspend services if payment is more than 45 days overdue.

4. Intellectual Property

4.1 All custom software, documentation, and deliverables created specifically for Client under this Agreement shall become the exclusive property of Client upon full payment.

4.2 Provider retains ownership of all pre-existing tools, libraries, and methodologies used in the project.

4.3 Provider grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use any Provider-owned components incorporated into the deliverables.

5. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information exchanged during the course of this Agreement. This obligation shall survive termination of the Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

6. Warranty

Provider warrants that all services shall be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards. Provider shall correct any defects in deliverables reported within ninety (90) days of acceptance at no additional cost to Client.

7. Limitation of Liability

In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages. Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client.

8. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice. In the event of termination, Client shall pay for all services rendered through the termination date.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California without regard to conflicts of law principles. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in San Francisco, CA.
